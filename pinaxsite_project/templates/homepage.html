An open-source platform built on the Django Web Framework.

By integrating numerous reusable Django apps and providing starter projects and infrastructure tools, Pinax takes care of the things that many sites have in common so you can focus on what makes your site different.

Pinax Features

Standard Project Layout

for consistency and easy deployment

Starter Projects

that can be used as the basis for any Django website as well as some tailored to community sites, company sites, intranets and sites in closed beta

Reusable Apps

providing both back-end functionality and user-facing components

Default Templates

to enable quick prototyping

...Including Apps for:

  • account management
    • openid
    • e-mail verification
    • password management
  • profiles
  • notifications
  • activity streams
  • private betas / waiting lists
  • badges
  • tagging
  • wikis
  • forums
  • blogs
  • task tracking
  • friend and follower relations
and more...

Developer Information

Pinax is available under an MIT license. The source code is available from GitHub.

Tickets and Wiki are at code.pinaxproject.com

Most discussion about Pinax takes place on the IRC channel #pinax on Freenode or the pinax-users mailing list on Google Groups.
